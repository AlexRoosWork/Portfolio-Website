TipXMR

2021-03-01 :: [Alex Roos]
#javascript  #typescript  #react  #crypto 
Check the project out on GitHub! TipXMR is by far the biggest and most complex project I have worked on so far. It all started small, in September of 2020 with the idea to support Monero donations in livestreams. Monero is the biggest privacy focused cryptocurrency in the world and a favorite of mine. The goal was to empower streamers to be less dependant on centralized services like YouTube or Twitch and to expand the use-case for Monero.
